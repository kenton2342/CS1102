Proof-of-Stake (PoS)



In contrast, Proof-of-Stake (PoS) offers a more energy-efficient alternative. Instead of mining, participants are known as validators, are selected to propose and validate blocks based on the amount of cryptocurrency they "stake" as collateral.



Ethereum employs a randomized selection process that also includes penalties for dishonest behavior. PoS reduces energy consumption by over 99% compared to PoW and promotes broader participation (OSL, 2025). Nonetheless, concerns remain about the concentration of wealth, as those with larger stakes have greater influence over the network, raising questions about long-term decentralization and security.
